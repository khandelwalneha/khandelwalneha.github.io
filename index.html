About Us

We are new generation of actor-creator-entrepreneurs professionally trained to perform and create work to a higher global standard of storytelling. We work with a faculty comprising of the best actors, playwrights, directors and producers.

Our Philosophy

Acting is the least mysterious of all crafts. Whenever we want something from somebody or when we want to hide something or pretend, we're acting. Most people do it all day long.

Mission & Vision

To create, develop and apply new acting tools/exercises inspired by elements of the fantastic. To inspire the actor’s creative imagination through exercises in Fantastic Realism.

Our Value

Acting with courage, challenging the status quo and finding new ways to grow our company and each other. Ignite a passion and encourages an environment for the actor to find their deepest potential, empowering truth with each new performance.
